    <h2 id="features">Features</h2>
    <p><strong>Menu Screen:</strong> The game starts with a menu screen offering three options:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Tilt Mode: Control Mario's movements and speed by tilting your device.</li>
        <li>Arrows Mode: Control Mario's movements using on-screen arrows.</li>
        <li>Top Score Screen: View a list of top scores achieved by players, and a map the mark the location in which the record were taken.</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>Tilt Mode</h3>
    <p>In Tilt Mode, players can control Mario's speed by tilting their device left and right, forward or backward. Tilting the device forward increases Mario's speed, while tilting it backward returns him to normal speed. Note: the phone must be held in a 90 degree angle</p>

    <h3>Arrows Mode</h3>
    <p>Players can opt for a more traditional control scheme by using on-screen arrows to maneuver Mario through the game. This mode provides familiarity for players accustomed to classic platformer controls.</p>

    <h3>Top Score Screen</h3>
    <p>The top score screen displays a list of records achieved by players. Tapping on a record reveals the location where the record was set, allowing players to strive for their personal bests.</p>

    <h2 id="usage">Usage</h2>
    <h3 id="menu-screen">Menu Screen</h3>
    <p>Upon launching the game, players are greeted with a menu screen presenting three options: Tilt Mode, Arrows Mode, and Top Score Screen. Players can select their preferred mode by tapping on the corresponding option.</p>

    <h3 id="tilt-mode">Tilt Mode</h3>
    <p>In Tilt Mode, players control Mario's speed by tilting their device forward or backward. Tilting the device forward increases Mario's speed, while tilting it backward returns him to normal speed. Players must navigate through the game's challenges while mastering the tilt mechanic to achieve high scores.</p>

    <h3 id="arrows-mode">Arrows Mode</h3>
    <p>Arrows Mode offers players a more traditional control scheme, with on-screen arrows for maneuvering Mario through the game. This mode provides precise control over Mario's movements, allowing players to navigate through obstacles with accuracy and skill.</p>

    <h3 id="top-score-screen">Top Score Screen</h3>
    <p>The Top Score Screen displays a list of records achieved by players, showcasing their highest scores and achievements. Tapping on a record reveals the location where the record was set, offering insight into the game's challenges and encouraging players to surpass their previous accomplishments.</p>
